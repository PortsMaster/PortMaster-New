## Controls

| Button | Action |
|--|--| 
|D-pad/L-stick|movement |
|A|jump/ while in the air spring jump|
|B|jump|
|X/L2|pickup spear/poke |
|Y/R2|Aim and throw Spear|
|R1|spring jump (press in the air)|
|Start|pause|
