## Controls
On devices without the right stick, you can still play by selecting "autofire" at the start of the run.

| Button | Action |
|--|--| 
|DPAD / Left stick|Movement|
|Right stick| Aiming|
|A|Confirm|
|B|Cancel / Single-use item|
|X|Bomb|
|Y|Activable|
|L1|Dash|
|Select|Menu|
|Start|Start game|
