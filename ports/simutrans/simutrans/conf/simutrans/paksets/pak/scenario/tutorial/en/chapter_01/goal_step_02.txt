<p>It is recommended to turn on the grid for this tutorial by using the <em>"#" key</em>.In addition, it is highly recommended that Trees are set to transparent, either through the settings menu, or by pressing the <em>"%" key</em>.</p><p>You can <em>zoom</em> in and out using either the Mouse wheel, or by using the <em>"Page Up/Page Down Keys"</em>.</p><p>You can <em>move</em> about the map either by holding down the Right Mouse button and dragging, using the Arrow Keys or the number pad.</p><p>You can <em>rotate</em> the map by pressing <em>"[Shift]+r" (Capital "R")</em> or in from toolbar select <em>Rotate Map</em> tool.</p><p>Try zooming in and out and moving about the map.</p><p><st>To proceed to the next step</st>, rotate the map.</p>
