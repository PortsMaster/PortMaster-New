<title>Map enlargement</title>

<h1><strong>Enlarge map</strong></h1>

<p>Here, one can extend the map to the right and the bottom. If one wants to extend to the left / top, one has to rotate the map first, enlarge then, and rotate back.
<br>
A preview of the enlarged map is shown in the top-right of the dialoge window.
</p>
