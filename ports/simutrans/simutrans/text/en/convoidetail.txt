<title>Convoy Details</title>

<h1><strong>Convoy Details</strong></h1>

<p>
<strong>Convoy Details</strong> provides more information about a Convoy (an operational vehicle or combination of vehicle-units) and can be used to sell the Convoy immediately.
</p>
<p>

<strong>Convoy Details</strong> can be opened from the
<a href="convoiinfo.txt">
Details</a> option-button in Convoy Information</strong>.
<br>
If not all vehicle-units are visible <a href="mouse.txt">re-size</a> <strong>Convoy Details</strong> or use the slider-bars to scroll.
</p>
<p>
At the top of

<strong>Convoy Details</strong>, the information shown includes:
</p>
<p>
<em>Power:</em> of Convoy (combined power of all vehicle-units in Convoy).
</p>
<p>
<em>Resale Value:</em> money received if Convoy is sold (purchase price of Convoy less any depreciation).
</p>
<p>
WARNING: Click on <em>Sell mode</em> option-button to sell Convoy immediately. No further confirmation is required. The Convoy is removed from the game and the player balance credited with the selling price.
</p>
<p>
<strong>Convoy Details</strong> lists all the vehicle-units in Convoy.<br>
Information listed for each vehicle-units includes:<br>
- <em>vehicle-unit-graphic</em> a picture of the vehicle-unit (also used in <a href="depot.txt">depots</a>)<br>
- <em>Manufactured:</em> month and year of manufacture, and introduction date if playing with <a href="new_world.txt">timeline</a><br>
- <em>Resale Value:</em> amount received if sold<br>
- <em>Power:</em> power generated (by motorised vehicle-units)<br>
- <em>Friction:</em> current friction level<br>
- <em>Max income:</em> Maximum possible income of this vehicle-unit based on type of item transported by this Convoy<br>
- <em>items on board</em> amount and destination of items currently carried by this vehicle-unit.
</p>
