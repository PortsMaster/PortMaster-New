<title>City List</title>

<h1><strong>City List</strong></h1>

<p>
The <strong>City List</strong> provides information about <a href="citywindow.txt">cities</a>. This window consists of two separate tabs: one for a list of cities and another for charts.<br>
Open the list by clicking on <a href="citylist_filter.txt">City List</a> in the <a href="list.txt">Lists</a> menu or by pressing <a href="keys.txt">[T]</a>.<br>
</p>
<p>
- <em>Total inhabitants:</em> the total number of citizens on the map (the total population of all cities). Recent increase in population is shown in brackets.
</p>
<p>
The <em>City List</em> tab lists every city on the map, displaying their names, population and <a href="citygrowth.txt">growth factor</a>.
</p>
<p>
<em>Sorted by:</em> provides options on how the list is sorted.
</p>
<p>
- <em>Name</em> sorts the list alphabetically by city name.<br>
- <em>Citizens</em> sorts the list by population.<br>
- <em>City Growth</em> sorts the list by growth rate.
</p>
<p>
- <em>ascending</em> / <em>descending</em> reverses the order of the list.
</p>
<p>
Click on an item in the list to reveal more <a href="citywindow.txt">information</a> about that city.<br>
</p>
<p>
The <em>Charts</em> tab provides information about the cities collectively.
</p>
<p>
- <em>Citizens</em>: total population of all cities (same as <em>Total inhabitants</em>).<br>
- <em>City Growth</em>: total combined <a href="citygrowth.txt">city growth</a> of all cities.<br>
- <em>Cities</em>: total number of cities on the map.<br>
- <em>Factories</em>: total number of <a href="industry_info.txt">factories</a> on the map.<br>
- <em>Convoys</em>: total number of transport vehicles.<br>
- <em>City Cars</em>: total number of city cars.<br>
- <em>Pass. Ratio</em>: percentage of total passengers transported.<br>
- <em>Passengers</em>: total number passengers generated (including those who have not been transported).<br>
- <em>Sent Mail</em>: percentage of total mail delivered.<br>
- <em>Mail</em>: total mail generated (including that which has not been transported).<br>
- <em>Arrived</em>: the number of times the storage of every factory was checked where that storage was not empty.<br>
- <em>Goods</em>: the number of times the storage of every factory was checked.<br>
</p>
